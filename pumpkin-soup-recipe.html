Pumpkin Soup

Pumpkin soup is a savory dish made from pureed pumpkin, typically blended with broth, cream, or coconut milk to achieve a smooth consistency. It is seasoned with various herbs and spices, such as cinnamon, nutmeg, or ginger, to enhance its flavor. Pumpkin soup is a popular fall and winter dish, known for its rich, warming taste. It can be garnished with toppings like seeds, croutons, or a drizzle of cream for added texture and visual appeal.

Prep Time

15 min.

Cook Time

35 min.

Serving

4-6 servings

Instructions

step 1

Cut the pumpkin into 3cm / 2.25" slices. Cut the skin off and scrape seeds out (video is helpful). Cut into 4cm / 1.5" chunks.

step 2

Place the pumpkin, onion, garlic, broth and water in a pot - liquid won't quite cover all the pumpkin. Bring to a boil, uncovered, then reduce heat and let simmer rapidly until pumpkin is tender (check with butter knife) - about 10 minutes.

step 3

Remove from heat and use a stick blender to blend until smooth (Note 3 for blender).

step 4

Season to taste with salt and pepper, stir through cream (never boil soup after adding cream, cream will split).

step 5

Ladle soup into bowls, drizzle over a bit of cream, sprinkle with pepper and parsley if desired. Serve with crusty bread!

Ingredients

1.2 kg / 2.4 lb pumpkin (any type) OR butternut squash, unpeeled weight

1 onion

2 garlic cloves

3 cups vegetable or chicken broth/stock

1 cup water

Salt and pepper

1/2 - 3/4 cup cream (at the end)

Tools

Blender

Measuring Spoons

Measuring Cups
